How we do it

Years ago, painting on any type of clothing material was almost imposible due to the lack of paint type that doesn't wash away. Recently, paints that are water proof have become accessible and that's excacly how we're able to paint on clothing!

Our customers ussually send us an image or a photo of the theme they wish illustrated on a desired type of clothing, after we agree on the theme and price painting can being!

Once the illustration is complete we'll be shipping it to you as soon as possible!

Process

Step 1

You send us the desired image of theme you wish illustrated or you explain what you want by writing it to us.

Step 2

This is when we get our hands dirty! In the meantime we like to bake cookies and try to keep our dogs from eating all the paint.

Step 3

The piece is finished and ready to be shipped! We agree on the shipping & billing method that works best for you and you get the piece as soon as possible.
